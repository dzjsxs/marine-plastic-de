Product Introduction

Nylon Sprockets, manufactured from cast PA6 or extruded PA66, are precision power transmission components designed to replace metal sprockets in applications where weight reduction, noise dampening, and corrosion resistance are priorities. The inherent self-lubricating properties of nylon significantly reduce maintenance requirements and extend service intervals.

Nylon sprockets are particularly valued in food processing, pharmaceutical, and packaging industries where metal contamination must be avoided and hygiene standards are paramount. Their lower moment of inertia compared to steel sprockets enables faster acceleration and deceleration in automated systems, improving overall production efficiency. Jiasheng Plastic manufactures nylon sprockets to standard chain pitch specifications (ANSI, DIN, ISO) and custom designs per customer drawings.
